A Personal Commitment to Elgar Ward

When my grandmother shared her concerns about the degradation of essential support services for older residents in our community, it struck a deep chord with me. While I can assist her with daily tasks, many of her friends aren't as fortunate. This personal experience opened my eyes to how council decisions directly impact the most vulnerable among us.

Rooted in Our Community

I've lived my entire life in Box Hill, attending Box Hill High School and cherishing the vibrant, diverse community that Elgar Ward offers. I understand firsthand the unique qualities that make our area specialâ€”our lush green spaces, thriving local businesses, and strong community bonds.

Challenges We Face

However, our beloved community is at a crossroads. Overdevelopment is threatening the character of Elgar Ward, with developers pushing high-density apartments that lack the space, light, and livability we value. Essential services like Aqualink are at risk of privatization, and the council's lack of transparency leaves residents in the dark about decisions that affect us all.

A Vision for the Future

I believe that with thoughtful planning and decisive action, we can preserve what makes Elgar Ward special while fostering a sustainable and inclusive future. Here's how we can achieve this together:

Key Initiatives

  • Promote sustainability by introducing a local soft-plastic recycling program to protect our environment for future generations.
  • Protect essential services by keeping Aqualink public to ensure it remains accessible to everyone in our community.
  • Defend our green spaces by opposing inappropriate developments and preventing the sale of council land to developers.
  • Improve infrastructure by repairing potholes promptly and enhancing road safety measures to accommodate our growing population.
  • Enhance public safety by increasing street lighting so residents feel secure in public areas at night.
  • Ensure transparency by bringing greater accountability to the council, reducing confidential meetings, and involving the community in decision-making.

Why I'm Running

As an IT professional and a lifelong resident of Box Hill, I bring both fresh perspectives and a deep understanding of our community's needs. I'm committed to finding innovative solutions to improve service delivery and to standing up for the values we all share.

Join Me in Making a Difference

Our community deserves a representative who not only listens but acts decisively to protect and enhance our quality of life. Over the coming weeks and months, I'll be knocking on doors and connecting with as many residents as possible because your thoughts and ideas are crucial.

Together, let's create a more inclusive, safe, and vibrant future for Elgar Ward.

Your Voice Matters

I invite you to share your concerns and hopes for our community. Let's work together to ensure that Elgar Ward remains a place we're all proud to call home.

A Better Vision for Elgar Ward

Elgar Ward is a vibrant and diverse community. With thoughtful planning, we can preserve and enhance the quality of life for all residents. I believe our community deserves a strong representative who not only listens but also takes decisive action to protect what makes Elgar special.

As someone who has lived in Box Hill my entire life, I am committed to preserving our green spaces, supporting local businesses, and ensuring our infrastructure continues to thrive.

Over the coming weeks and months, I’ll be knocking on doors and connecting with as many residents as possible. Your thoughts and ideas are crucial to shaping the future of our community.

Together, let’s create a more inclusive, safe, and vibrant future for Elgar Ward.

Protecting Our Community

We are seeing too many developers profiting from over-supplying cheap, high-density apartments that don’t meet our community's need for space, light, and livability.

I’ve lived my whole life in this area, and I am passionate about maintaining our lively, family-friendly neighborhood.

With the right planning, we can keep the Box Hill we know and love – with green spaces, great community infrastructure, and thriving local businesses that support our diverse community.

Key Initiatives

Here are the key issues I will focus on as your representative:

    Introduce a local soft-plastic recycling plan to promote sustainability.
    Protect Aqualink from privatization, keeping essential services accessible for all.
    Defend council green spaces and council services from encroaching development.
    Demand high livability standards for new residential apartments to ensure they meet community needs for space, light, and parking.
    Repair potholes and improve road safety across Elgar Ward.
    Increase street lighting to improve safety in public areas.
    Bring greater accountability to the council, ensuring transparency in all decisions.

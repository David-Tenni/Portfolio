A Better Vision for Elgar Ward

‘Elgar’ is a vibrant and diverse community. With careful planning, we can maintain and improve the quality of life for all residents. Our community needs a strong representative who listens to every voice and takes decisive action with a long-term vision.

In the coming weeks and months, I’ll be out and about, knocking on doors and talking with as many of you as possible. I want to hear your thoughts.

Join me in shaping a more inclusive, vibrant, and thriving future together, with A Strong Voice for Elgar Ward.

Protecting Our Community

Too many developers are profiting from over-supplying cheap, high-density shoebox apartments that don’t support residents’ needs for light, space, and parking.

I’ve lived my whole life in Box Hill, and I love our lively, family-friendly area. With thoughtful planning, we can maintain the Box Hill we love – with green spaces, great community infrastructure, and thriving local businesses.

Key Initiatives

Initiate a local soft-plastic recycling plan.

Protect Aqualink from privatization.

Defend council green spaces, open space, and council services.

Demand high livability standards for new residential apartments.
